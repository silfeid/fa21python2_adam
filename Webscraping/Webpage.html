The Origin of the Snickerdoodle

The origins of the popular traditional American cookie known as the "snickerdoodle" are shrouded in mystery, making this confection among the most enigmatic known to the culinary world.

Candidates for Region of Origin

  • Germany
  • New England
  • Pennsylvania Dutch Country

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon

Pennsylvania Dutch Origin Thesis

The Pennsylvania Dutch, stem, of course, not from the Netherlands at all, but rather from Germany, "Dutch" being derived from "Deutsch," the endonym used by speakers of Low and High German alike.
